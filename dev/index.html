Thermochron.SimAnnealSigma — Method
SimAnnealSigma(n, AnalyticalSigma, InitialUncertainty, ModelUncertainty)

To avoid getting stuck in local optima, decrease uncertainty slowly by simulated annealing: Calculate annealing sigma which declines from InitialUncertainty to ModelUncertainty with a decay constant of lambda. Returns:

sigma = sqrt(AnalyticalSigma^2 + AnnealingSigma^2)
